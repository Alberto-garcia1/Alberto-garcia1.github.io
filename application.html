Welcome to this Embedded Web Application

This page embeds an interactive web application for you to play. Below, you'll find instructions on how to use it along some additional information.

About the Application

The embedded application is called basketabll-io. The goal is to score a basketball against the opponent. It's a great way to pass time while having fun.

How to Play

  • Use Mouse: Click and drag your character to move them around.
  • Avoid Other Players: Runnung into other players will result in you losing the basketball.
  • Reach The Bastet In order to win, you must reach the basket where your character will score.

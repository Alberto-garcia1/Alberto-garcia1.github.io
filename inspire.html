What makes a hero?

People often ask what makes a hero a hero. I think that definition is very personal. You decide what it take to be a hero and you become it.

Over the last 100 years no one exemplifies these values for me more than Martin Luther King. He called on people to be accountable for the racial prejudices they carried. He demonstrated that violence is not the best way to create lasting change. And throughout his life he sacrificed everything to make the world better.
